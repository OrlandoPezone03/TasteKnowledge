Why this recipe works

🧬 Flour Chemistry

  • Composition: mainly starch and proteins such as glutenin and gliadin.
  • Function: The interaction between liquid and flour creates gluten strands which trap air bubbles, making the pancakes fluffy.

🌡️ Maillard Reaction

The browning you see on the surface is due to the Maillard reaction, happening between amino acids and reducing sugars around 140°C.
